Meat Types

Asada - Stake: Carne asada is sirloin steak sliced, cooked with a marinade and some searing to impart a charred flavor.

Al pastor - Marinated Pork:Al pastor is a tender slice of pork cut in small pieces. its pork is marinate with spices and other ingredients.

Chorizo:Chorizo is a home made chorizo, mixture of chopped pork meat and spices.

Birria - Slow Cook Beef:Birria is slow cook beef with chile and spices.

Milanesa - Breaded Steak:Milanesa is a breaded steak with spices.

Pierna - Ham:Pierna is smoke ham.

About Us:

As a native from Mexico City, raise in California. I have always lived around small food business, and love the joy they brought to me and my peers. That’s why my family has decided to bring the same JOY! to Albuquerque New Mexico a taste of the southern borders. We have infuse Michuancan and Mexico City food cuisine. Los Huaraches de Villa is a food truck that provides Mexico City street food, such as Tacos de Canasta, Huaraches, Panbasos, Tortas, Tacos and Birria Michouacan style.
