Meat Types

Asada - Stake: Carne asada is sirloin steak sliced, cooked with a marinade and some searing to impart a charred flavor.

Al pastor - Marinated Pork:Al pastor is a tender slice of pork cut in small pieces. its pork is marinate with spices and other ingredients.

Chorizo:Chorizo is a home made chorizo, mixture of chopped pork meat and spices.

Birria - Slow Cook Beef:Birria is slow cook beef with chile and spices.

Milanesa - Breaded Steak:Milanesa is a breaded steak with spices.

Pierna - Ham:Pierna is smoke ham.
