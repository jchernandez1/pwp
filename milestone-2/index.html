Los Huaraches de Villa is a food truck that provides Mexico City street food, such as Tacos de Canasta, Huaraches, Panbasos, Tortas, Tacos and Birria Michuacan style.

Step 1: Chose from the menu

  • Tacos: Come with your choice of meat, Cilantro and Onion.
  • Huaraches: Come with your choice of meat, beans, lettuce, sour cream, and cheese.
  • Tortas: Comes with your choice of meat, beans, lettuce, tomato, avocado, and toasted Oxaca cheese.
  • Pambasos: Comes with your choice of meat, lettuce, sour cream, and cheese.
  • Burritos: Comes with your choice of meat, beans, lettuce, and cheese.
  • Quesadilla: Comes with your choice of meat, cheese and a side salad with avocado, tomato.

Step 2: Chose the type of meat you like

  • Carne Asada - Steak

    • Carne asada is sirloin steak sliced, cooked with a marinade and some searing to impart a charred flavor.

  • Al Pastor - Marinated Pork

    • Al pastor is a tender slice of pork cut in small pieces. its pork is marinate with spices and other ingredients.

  • Home made Chorizo

    • Chorizo is a mixture of chopped pork meat and spices, which produce its characteristic deep red color.

  • Birria - Slow Cook Beef

  • Milanesa - Breaded Steak

  • Pierna o Jamon - Ham
